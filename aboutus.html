ABOUT US

HaSSan Constructors (Pvt) is the premier construction and engineering services company of Pakistan. HaSSan constructor is licensed by the Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC), the regulatory body for construction and engineering services, in the highest license category (CA - unlimited project execution size). While most of the construction projects in Pakistan are modeled on the measurement-based, build-to-design (item-rate) contracts, Astral has performed perhaps the largest number and highest value of Design-Build firm-fixed-priced construction projects in the country!


From its inception, the company has been very selective in its human resource hiring and is focused on strengthening them through off-site and on-the-job training. The company has developed its internal processes modeled after the best-in-class for planning, execution and safety.
